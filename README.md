# 🏥 Clinic & Pharmacy Management System

A modern, lightweight **Clinic & Pharmacy Management Web App** designed for small clinics, medical stores, and pharmacies.  
Built with simplicity, speed, and usability in mind — no backend required.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Dashboard
- Daily sales overview
- Profit tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Expiry notifications

### 💊 Medicine & Stock Management
- Add medicines with batch, expiry & supplier
- Auto stock calculation (boxes → strips)
- Low stock alerts
- Expiry tracking system

### 🩺 Prescription & Billing
- Create patient prescriptions
- Add multiple medicines with dosage
- Extra charges (tests, consultation)
- Discount system (%, fixed)
- Generate:
  - Rx + Cash Bill
  - Rx + Credit Bill
  - Rx Only

### 👥 Patients & Credit System
- Patient records with phone & WhatsApp
- Credit tracking (udhaar system)
- Payment history
- Due reminders (WhatsApp/SMS ready)

### 🏢 Supplier Management
- Supplier records
- Payment tracking
- EasyPaisa / Bank details support

### 📋 Orders System
- Create supplier orders
- Track pending orders
- Add multiple items per order

### 💸 Expense Management
- Daily expenses tracking
- Categories (rent, electricity, etc.)
- Financial summary

### 📊 Reports
- Monthly & yearly reports
- Profit insights
- Charts (Chart.js)

### 💾 Backup & Security
- Export data (JSON backup)
- Restore data anytime
- Password protection for:
  - Reports
  - Data reset

---

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3 (Modern UI / Responsive)**
- **Vanilla JavaScript**
- **Chart.js**

No frameworks. No database. Fully client-side.
